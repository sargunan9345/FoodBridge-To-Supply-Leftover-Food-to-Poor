### **1. Objective:**

To define a clear plan for developing, implementing, and monitoring the FoodBridge system. This includes setting project goals, tasks, timelines, responsibilities, and deliverables to ensure smooth execution.

---

### **2. Project Scope:**

The FoodBridge project focuses on creating a platform that connects food donors with NGOs and volunteers to reduce food wastage and hunger.
**In Scope:**

* User registration (Donor, NGO, Volunteer)
* Real-time food posting and pickup tracking
* Notifications and delivery confirmation

**Out of Scope:**

* Payment systems
* International food delivery logistics
